Welcome to My Personal Webpage

Hey there! My name is Henrique, and I am currently a PhD candidate in Earth Sciences at Roma Tre University in Rome, Italy. My current research focuses on using seismological constraints to unveil the temperature and composition of the crust. I have experience in previous research on seismic attenuation, seismic tomography, rock physics, paleomagnetism, and stratigraphy. Here, you can find information about my research, publications, grants, and more. I am currently in my last year of PhD (finishing in December) and I am searching for a Post-Doc. Feel free to contact me by email. 😃
