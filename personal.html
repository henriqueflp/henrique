Nice to meet you! 🌎 🌈

What's up? My name is Henrique (pronounciation /ẽˈʁiki/) and I was born on a Brazilian island in the Atlantic Ocean called Florianópolis. After spending 25 years growing up in this surfing and nature paradise, I decided to embark on new adventures across the Atlantic. I am currently pursuing my PhD in Earth Sciences at Roma Tre University in Rome, Italy. When I'm not immersed in seismic data and geological structures, I love exploring the Eternal City, traveling to new countries, engaging in photography, playing with my dogs 🐕 (I have two back in BR), and spending quality time with friends and family.

Universities have always been my passion. Throughout my nine years of geological studies, I've had the opportunity to study at four different universities, each offering unique environments and opportunities to make new friends. I completed my bachelor's degree in geology at UFSC and a master's degree in Marine Geology at UFRGS, located a bit further south in Brazil. During my bachelor's studies, I spent a semester in Norway, studying Geophysics at the University of Bergen. It was there that my passion for geophysics truly blossomed.

My favorite hobbies are watching Formula One 🏁 and learning new languages!
