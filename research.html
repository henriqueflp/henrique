My current research focuses on applying seismic methods to uncover the heterogeneities of the crust. I am utilizing ambient noise tomography and receiver functions to study the European crust. Additionally, the intriguing phenomenon of seismic wave attenuation captivates me, and I have been exploring it, jointly with seismic velocities to reveal the thermochemical and structural properties of the crust.

Research Interests

  • Physical properties of the crust
  • Passive methods on seismic tomography
  • Attenuation of seismic waves
  • Machine learning
  • Data analysis
